Mörenschnitten mit Frischkäse-Zuckerguss

Elkes Mörenschnitten

Here is a simple and excellent recipe of carrot cake with a cream cheese glaze

Ingredients and instructions carrot cake

  • 4 eggs
  • 200g sugar
  • 200ml sunflower oil
  • 190g carrot puree
    • 171g organically grown carrots
    • 19ml water

      • make a puree

stir until frothy

  • 230g flour
  • 2 tsp (=teaspoon) baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp salt

mix and add to the previous mass

bake at 170°C for 30 - 40 minutes

if you use a baking tray than double the ingredients

Ingredients and instructions cream cheese glaze

  • 200g cream cheese (or more)
  • 25ml milk
  • 90g powder sugar
  • 1 packet of vanilla sugar

mix everything evenly to a cream and spread it on the cake

add cocoa powder and/or almonds or walnut on top if you so desire
