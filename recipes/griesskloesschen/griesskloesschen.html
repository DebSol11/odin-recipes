Griesskloesschen

Margits Griesskloesschen

Here is a simple but delicious recipe of griesskloesschen. They can be eaten like that or added to a variety of dishes like a soup shown in the picture.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp (= tablespoon) margarine
  • 2 eggs
  • 8,5 tbsp semolina
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of nutmeg
  • 1 tip of a knife baking powder

Instructions

  1. Mix everything together
  2. Let it sit for 30 minutes
  3. Cook for 5 minutes
  4. Let it sit again before you enjoy
